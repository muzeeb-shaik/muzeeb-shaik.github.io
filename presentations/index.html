Experiential Learning & Gender Stereotypes in Science Education

with Huanhuan Shi, Adithya Pattabhiramaiah & Hari Sridhar
Evidence from a field intervention in Bodoland, Assam — a Mobile Science Lab program with 5,216 students across 193 schools. The deck covers the field DiD results (≈ 20% reduction in "science is for boys" endorsement), heterogeneity by student gender and school size, and two follow-up mechanism studies: a scenario experiment with 151 Prolific adults and an EDSL synthetic-participant replication with 450 LLM agents.
